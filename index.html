Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and medical information. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your explicit consent.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services: name, contact details, medical history, and appointment data. All collection is transparent and with your consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data at any time. You can also request a copy of all information we hold about you.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may be limited.

Data Sharing

We never sell your data. Information is shared only with your healthcare providers and only for the purpose of delivering care.

Security Measures

We employ multiple layers of security including SSL encryption, regular security audits, and strict access controls to protect your data.
